Rabbit Meat Cuts

At Nature's Protein, each cut of rabbit meat serves a unique purpose in our delicious products. The Ribs and Shank are carefully seasoned and ground to create our famous Rabbit Longaniza, blending traditional spices for a unique twist. Lastly, the tender Saddle and Loin cuts are finely chopped and used in our deliciously crispy Rabbit Sisig, a local favorite packed with flavor.
